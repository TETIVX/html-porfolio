Ernie's Fall Favorites

Best October Frights

Night of the Living Dead (1990)

Night of the living dead is a classic with all of the classic horror movie elements.

Evil Dead (2013)

Old school gory effects make this remake an awesome watch for Halloween. This movie does a great job of keeping the original campy feel, but ties in an internal conflict as well.

The Blair Witch Project (1999)

This movie is a true psychological horror masterpiece. The found-footage genre in all its glory. This is a movie that delivers for frights and feels.
